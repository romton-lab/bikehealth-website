3. Service description

BikeHealth is an Android application designed to help you track maintenance of personal bicycles. It allows you to, among other things:

  • Register one or more bikes and their components.
  • Track component wear based on kilometres ridden.
  • Receive notifications when maintenance thresholds are approached.
  • Manually enter or automatically sync (via Strava) your trips.
  • Log maintenance operations carried out.

The App works without a user account and without a central server. All user data is stored locally on your device. Only anonymous crash reports are sent to Firebase (Google) to identify real-world bugs, which can be disabled at any time in the App's settings. See the Privacy policy for details.
