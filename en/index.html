Features

Built to anticipate, not to repair.

Made for cyclists who want to understand their bike without becoming a mechanic.

Component tracking

Chain, cassette, brake pads, tyres, chainring, wheels, battery and motor (e-bike)… Track every part with its own custom wear thresholds.

Recurring trips

Set your routines (commute, Sunday rides…) and BikeHealth generates your recurring trips automatically.

Strava sync

Connect your Strava account and your bike activities are imported automatically. Mileage updates itself.

Maintenance reminders

Lube the chain, check tyre pressure, inspect brakes, annual service: BikeHealth sends the right reminder at the right time.

Simple or Advanced mode

Casual rider or weekend mechanic: choose your level of detail. The app adapts to you, not the other way around.

Favourite shop

Save your trusted mechanic: their hours and phone number are one tap away from every bike profile. Essential on those days when something's creaking.

How it works

Five steps to ride with peace of mind.

Set up your profile

Pick your mode (Simple or Advanced) and your weekly reminder day. Three minutes, no more.

Add your bikes

Brand, model, mileage, photo. Brand new or second-hand, BikeHealth adapts.

Set your routines

Commute, Sunday ride, Saturday errands. Or connect Strava and forget about it.

Ride with peace of mind

BikeHealth alerts you when a component nears its threshold or maintenance is due. Not before.

Log your maintenance

When you swap a chain or lube the drivetrain, log it in two taps. BikeHealth recalculates, the cycle continues.
