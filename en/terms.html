In one sentence: BikeHealth is an ad-free app, provided as-is by an independent developer. It helps you track your bike's wear but doesn't replace a mechanic or your common sense.

1. What BikeHealth is

BikeHealth is an Android app for tracking bike maintenance, developed and maintained as a personal, non-commercial project. It works without a user account and without a central server — everything is stored locally on your device. It will be distributed for free on Google Play.

For details on what is collected (crash reports, anonymous usage), see the Privacy policy.

2. Provided as-is

BikeHealth is a side project. The App is provided without warranty: it may have bugs, change, or stop at any time without notice. No liability is accepted for data loss following uninstallation, a device change, an update, or a malfunction — nor for unavailability of integrated third-party services (Strava, Google).

3. Bike safety

The wear thresholds offered are estimates based on manufacturer averages or common cycling conventions. They do not replace regular visual inspection, a professional mechanic's diagnosis, or the manufacturer's specific recommendations. You remain solely responsible for your bike's condition and your safety on every ride, whether or not the App emitted an alert.
